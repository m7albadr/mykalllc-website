Our Mission

Why We Exist

We believe nutrition tracking should feel effortless, not like homework. We're building technology that meets people where they are — speaking their language, understanding their food, and fitting into their daily life without friction.
01

Intelligence Over Input

Our AI handles the complexity so users don't have to. Speak, snap a photo, or type — we figure out the rest.

02

Culturally Aware

Built from day one for Arabic and English speakers with region-specific food databases and natural dialect support.

03

Privacy as Foundation

Health data is deeply personal. We architect for privacy first — your data belongs to you, full stop.

04

Behavior, Not Numbers

We focus on building sustainable habits, not obsessing over calories. Small consistent changes create lasting health.

Our Technology

Built on Modern AI Infrastructure

Every layer of our stack is designed for speed, accuracy, and cost-efficiency at scale.

Multi-Modal AI

Voice recognition, image analysis, and natural language processing working together to understand any meal input.

Real-Time Processing

Sub-second response times powered by edge functions and optimized inference pipelines for instant nutritional analysis.

Privacy Architecture

End-to-end encryption, minimal data retention, and zero third-party data sharing by design.

MK Assistant

A context-aware AI companion that adapts its personality — friendly, strict, or playful — based on user behavior patterns.

Regional Food Intelligence

Custom-trained models that understand diverse regional cuisines with cultural accuracy and local food database coverage.

Behavioral Analytics

Pattern recognition that tracks habits over time, delivering personalized insights and proactive health suggestions.

Our Approach

How We Build

We take a disciplined, user-first approach to product development. Every feature earns its place.

01

Behavior-Driven Design

We study real user behavior before writing a single line of code. Every interaction is designed around how people naturally think about food and health.

02

Ship, Measure, Iterate

We believe in rapid, disciplined releases. Ship small, measure real usage, iterate fast. No feature survives without earning engagement.

03

Cost-Conscious AI

We optimize every AI call for cost and speed. Smart model routing, aggressive caching, and lean inference keep our technology scalable and sustainable.
